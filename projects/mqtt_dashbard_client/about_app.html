MQTT Dashboard Client

A powerful app for controlling IoT devices via MQTT protocol

Designed for monitoring and controlling devices that support MQTT protocol, including:

Tasmota Sonoff Electrodragon ESP8266 Arduino Raspberry Pi Smart Home Sensors Thermostats
Download

Available in stores

Key Features

Background Operation

The app continues to operate in the background, providing constant monitoring of your devices.

Widget Grouping

Convenient organization of widgets into groups for better visualization and management.

Scenes

Create scenes to send messages to multiple devices simultaneously.

Multiple Brokers

Support for simultaneous operation with multiple MQTT brokers.

Backup

Create local backups to protect your settings and configuration.

JSONPath

JSONPath support for working with complex data structures.
